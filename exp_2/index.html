Study Tips

Pomodoro

Study for 25 mins, break for 5 mins. Keeps your brain focused and prevents burnout.

Active Recall

Test yourself constantly instead of just re-reading your notes passively.

No Distractions

Keep your phone in another room to maintain a deep state of concentration.
