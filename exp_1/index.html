Top Study Tips

1. Try the Pomodoro Technique

Study in focused bursts of 25 minutes, followed by a 5-minute break. This keeps your mind fresh and prevents burnout.

2. Practice Active Recall

Instead of just re-reading notes, test yourself. Close your book and try to write down everything you remember about the topic.

3. Eliminate Distractions

Put your phone in another room and close unnecessary tabs on your computer. A dedicated study environment improves concentration.

4. Utilize Great Resources

Don't hesitate to look outside your textbook. Websites like Khan Academy offer excellent, free explanations on various subjects.

5. Sleep and Hydrate

Your brain cannot retain information if you are exhausted or dehydrated. Aim for 8 hours of sleep and keep water at your desk.
